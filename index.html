Answers to Prayer and God’s Faithfulness

"This picture isn't exactly 'pretty,' but it’s our reality. This is our third trip to the bomb shelter per night as enemy drones target our district."

A Season of Rest: I am so grateful to God for the opportunity to spend a peaceful solo retreat in the winter mountains. It was a priceless time of silence and prayer that allowed me to recharge and listen for God’s voice before this next season of ministry.

Lana and Nazar’s Safe Return: We are finally reunited! Lana and Nazar have finally returned safely from Georgia. It was surprising and touching to hear Lana say how deeply she missed Ukraine. Even though we have no electricity or heat, and despite the constant shelling, she really wanted to be home.

A New Fire for Ministry: We are witnessing a unique spiritual awakening right now; both our staff and volunteers are exceptionally "on fire" for the mission. This renewed passion gives us the strength to push forward despite the external circumstances.

Gearing Up for a Busy Season

We truly feel the Lord is preparing something special for our youth. Here is how we are preparing for the new semester.

Seminary
Training

We started the year with a powerful boost: our team underwent seminary training focused on Pneumatology, Hamartiology, and Anthropology. This theological foundation gave us great momentum!

Strategic
Planning

We moved straight into planning for the rest of the winter, spring, and summer in Odesa. We spent a few days away at a retreat house for a special time of reunion and strategic alignment.

Packed
Semester

We have countless personal appointments, a major English Club project with an American team that has become like family to us, and even an upcoming mission trip to Kazakhstan!

Reaping a Long-Awaited Harvest

I want to share the stories of three young men I am mentoring, with whom I’ve built long-term relationships.

Yaroslav

Proactive Student

Joined our English Clubs back in spring 2025. After hearing the Gospel, he wasn't ready to go deeper initially. But recently, he reached out to me! He wants to explore Christianity, and we are currently having our third meeting dedicated to studying Scripture.

Ilya

Active Evangelist

Joined as a volunteer during our first English Club. He grew up in a Christian family but faced challenges. We are studying the Word together, and now, he is fully engaged in the ministry and is one of the most active evangelists and leaders on our team!

Seraphim

Orthodox Background

Son of a priest. We are going through a foundational study for new believers, discussing Salvation and a personal relationship with Christ. He can hardly wait to finish the course so he can join the team as a volunteer!

Veteran Ministry

Community Support

It’s not just about students. I also help out at veteran meetings. I’m truly happy to be part of a community of Christians who have gone through the war, kept their faith, and have so many plans for ministry.
